Privacy Policy

Prism Patch collects account details, profile preferences, project records, ideas, inventory entries, and community safety reports so the app can save your diamond painting studio and protect the community.

Date of birth and gender are used for account safety and personalization. Location sharing is optional and can be turned off in Settings. When off, your location is not shown in the app.

We do not sell personal information. This build does not use advertising trackers, third-party analytics, crash-reporting SDKs, or push notifications.

You can request account deletion in Settings. Deletion removes the account and personal saved data from the active app database.

Support contact: support@prismpatch.app
