Community Guidelines

Share diamond painting progress, finished canvases, storage ideas, and helpful tips with kindness.

Do not share abusive, hateful, explicit, threatening, stolen, spammy, or off-topic content. Use Report on a community post when something needs review, and Block when you do not want to see a member in your feed.

Reports are queued for moderation. Accounts that repeatedly violate these guidelines may lose access to community features.
